Tupolev Tu-154B-2
This version is not a simple port of the B-2 mod but a comprehensive rework of the aircraft’s flight dynamics, engine model and aircraft systems, leveraging X-Plane 12’s new Plane Maker capabilities. In particular, the automatic flight control system (ABSU), longitudinal balancing and pitch handling saw a major rework. All in all, the plane now matches the real aircraft’s flight characteristics much closer in both manual and automatic modes. To download it read the installation manual here.
